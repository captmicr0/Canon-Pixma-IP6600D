I have attempted to make a tray from the previously posted photo.  Had to scale the ruler close to actual size.   I think my dimensions are very close.  It does however spit out the tray and say no tray or no printable cd in tray.

But I would like to comment on an observation I have made.  When the tray is inserted and lined up with the arrows, the two "reflectors" are in line with the ink carriage.  The location appears to match the location of the prisms in the ink cartridges.  Furthermore the size of the "reflectors" is exaclty the same as the square base of the prisms on the ink cartridges.  I now believe that the same light used to read the ink level may be reading the "reflectors" and that they may in fact be prisms.


EDIT:  It appears that there is a sensor on each side of the ink head carriage which are spaced the same distance apart as the 2 reflectors.
