  • Blocking Plastic Removal

    fig-2-1 by Canon Australia

    Units that support CD/DVD-printing have the QM2-1239-000 inner cover unit w/ CDR. See fig 2-1.

    Units that do not support CD/DVD-printing have the QM2-1241-000 inner cover unit w/o CDR. See fig 2-2.

    These model numbers are specific to the iP4000 which are the same on the iP3000/5000/6000/8500 except for minor cosmetic differences. For example the “Inner Cover” on the iP3000 is gray and does not have the Canon logo in Chrome. The first step in CD printing on N.American models is removal of the blocking plastic of the inner cover simply done by opening the inner cover and using a small flathead screwdriver.

  • Roller installation (iP3000/4000/5000/6000D/8500 MP750/760/780 only)

The rollers are sold as part of an assembly including the inner door it self. See fig 2-4. In order to avoid printer disassembly it's recommended that you remove the guide roller assembly in the same way as you removed the blocking plastic from your printer. See fig 2-5.

Proper assembly is dependent on compressing the rollers (fig 2-8) before attaching the guide roller assembly to the inner cover. The best way to accomplish this task is to insert your CD/DVD tray first and apply the guide roller assembly at an angle to make sure you can see (fig 2-9).


Once you are sure the roller tabs are in the correct spot the simply apply pressure to the top of the guide roller assembly making sure that the round pegs go in the alignment holes and the clips snap firmly into the center square hole. See fig 2-10.
